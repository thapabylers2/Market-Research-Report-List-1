<p><ul><li>Solid-state AC Circuit Breaker</li><li>Hybrid AC Circuit Breaker</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AC Circuit Breaker market is primarily divided into two types: solid-state AC circuit breakers and hybrid AC circuit breakers. Solid-state AC circuit breakers utilize semiconductor technology to achieve fast interruption of current, offering high reliability and reduced maintenance. In contrast, hybrid AC circuit breakers combine features of both traditional electromechanical breakers and solid-state components, providing enhanced protection and flexibility in handling large fault currents while maintaining rapid response times. This segmentation addresses varying application needs in electrical systems.</p></p>
