<p><p>The air purification systems market encompasses various types, each utilizing distinct technologies. HEPA purifiers trap airborne particles with high efficiency, effectively removing dust, pollen, and allergens. Activated carbon purifiers absorb volatile organic compounds and odors. Static electricity purifiers use charged plates to capture particles from the air. Ion and ozone generator purifiers release ions to neutralize pollutants but require careful use due to potential ozone emission. Other market types include UV light purifiers and photocatalytic systems, which combat microorganisms and pollutants through different mechanisms.</p></p>
