<p><p>The animal insecticide market comprises three primary types: Endoparasiticides, Ectoparasiticides, and Endectocides. Endoparasiticides are designed to target internal parasites, such as worms and flukes, affecting livestock and pets. Ectoparasiticides focus on external parasites, like fleas, ticks, and mites, providing treatment for infestations on the skin and fur. Endectocides combine the benefits of both, effectively addressing both internal and external parasites in a single formulation. These products are essential for maintaining animal health and productivity.</p></p>
