<p><p>The auto fire extinguisher market includes various types of extinguishing agents tailored for specific fire risks. Dry chemical extinguishers use powdered agents to combat flammable liquid and electrical fires. Foam extinguishers create a barrier on flammable liquids, smothering flames and preventing re-ignition. Carbon dioxide extinguishers displace oxygen, effectively suffocating fires, particularly in enclosed spaces. Other types may include water, wet chemical, or clean agent systems, each designed to address unique fire scenarios and ensure effective fire suppression in vehicles.</p></p>
