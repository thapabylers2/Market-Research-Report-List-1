<p><strong>The Briquetter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Transmission</li><li>Hydraulic Transmission</li><li>Pneumatic Transmission</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The briquetter market is categorized into three main types based on transmission methods. Mechanical transmission briquetters utilize gears and belts for compacting materials, offering efficiency and durability. Hydraulic transmission systems employ hydraulic presses to generate high pressure, resulting in dense briquettes suitable for various applications. Pneumatic transmission relies on compressed air to operate the briquetting process, providing flexibility and speed. Each type offers distinct advantages, catering to specific industrial needs in briquette production.</p></p>
