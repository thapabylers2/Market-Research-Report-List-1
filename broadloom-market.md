<p><ul><li>Cut Pile</li><li>Loop Pile</li><li>Cut and Loop Pile</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The broadloom market is categorized into three main types: cut pile, loop pile, and cut and loop pile. Cut pile features fibers that are sheared, creating a soft, luxurious texture ideal for high-traffic areas. Loop pile consists of uncut loops, providing durability and resistance to wear, making it suitable for various settings. Cut and loop pile combines both techniques, offering a unique texture and design versatility, catering to aesthetic preferences while ensuring functionality in residential and commercial spaces.</p></p>
