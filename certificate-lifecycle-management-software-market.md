<p><p>Certificate Lifecycle Management (CLM) software is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based CLM software operates on remote servers, offering scalability, accessibility, and reduced maintenance costs. This model is ideal for organizations seeking flexibility and ease of updates. In contrast, on-premises CLM software is installed directly on a company's servers, providing greater control and security over sensitive data. This option suits organizations with strict compliance requirements or specific infrastructure needs. Both models facilitate efficient management of digital certificates throughout their lifecycle.</p></p>
