<p><ul><li>Dynamic Chemisorption Technique</li><li>Static Volumetric Chemisorption Technique</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Chemisorption analysis encompasses techniques to study the adsorption of molecules on solid surfaces. The Dynamic Chemisorption Technique involves continuous gas flow, allowing real-time monitoring of adsorption and desorption processes, which is crucial for analyzing reaction kinetics. In contrast, the Static Volumetric Chemisorption Technique measures changes in gas volume at equilibrium, providing insights into surface area and pore structure without ongoing flow. Both methods serve distinct purposes in material characterization, influencing applications in catalysis, materials science, and surface engineering.</p></p>
