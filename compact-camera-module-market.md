<p><p>The Compact Camera Module market encompasses various types, including Chip-on-Board (COB), Chip-on-Flex (COF), Flip Chip (FC), and other innovative packaging technologies. COB integrates the camera sensor directly onto a circuit board, enhancing compactness and performance. COF uses flexible substrates, providing design versatility and space-saving benefits. FC technology improves connectivity and thermal management by flipping the chip and connecting it directly to the substrate. Collectively, these types cater to diverse applications in smartphones, tablets, and automotive systems, driving market growth.</p></p>
