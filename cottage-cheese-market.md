<p><p>The cottage cheese market is categorized into three main types: natural cheese, processed cheese, and cheese food. Natural cheese consists of minimally processed dairy products, retaining the freshness and nutritional value of milk. Processed cheese includes products made by blending natural cheese with emulsifiers and other ingredients for a consistent texture and flavor. Cheese food is a blend of cheese, dairy ingredients, and additives, designed to offer a creamier texture and enhanced flavor profiles, often used in versatile culinary applications.</p></p>
