<p><p>The diagnostic specialty antibody market comprises three main types: monoclonal antibodies, polyclonal antibodies, and recombinant polyclonal antibodies. Monoclonal antibodies are uniform, targeting specific antigens, making them ideal for precise diagnostics. Polyclonal antibodies are a mixture of various antibodies from different immune cells, providing a broader response but less specificity. Recombinant polyclonal antibodies combine elements of both, engineered for enhanced specificity and activity, thus improving diagnostic accuracy and reliability across various clinical applications.</p></p>
