<p><p>Double walled water baths are widely used in laboratories for temperature control and gentle heating. They come in two primary market types: rectangular and round. Rectangular water baths offer efficient space utilization, making them ideal for fitting multiple samples or larger beakers. Both types cater to various research and industrial applications, ensuring versatility in laboratory settings.</p></p>
