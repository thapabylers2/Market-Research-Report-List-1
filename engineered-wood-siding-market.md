<p><strong>The Engineered Wood Siding Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oriented Strand Board</li><li>High-density Fiberboard</li><li>Veneered Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The engineered wood siding market comprises three main types: Oriented Strand Board (OSB), High-Density Fiberboard (HDF), and Veneered Plywood. OSB is made from wood strands bonded with adhesives, offering durability and resistance to moisture. High-Density Fiberboard, composed of wood fibers compressed under heat and pressure, provides a smooth surface ideal for painting. Veneered Plywood features thin layers of wood veneer glued onto a plywood core, combining aesthetic appeal with structural strength. Each type serves various applications in construction and remodeling, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
