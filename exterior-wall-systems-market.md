<p><ul><li>Ventilated Façade</li><li>Curtain Walls</li><li>Non-Ventilated Façade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Exterior wall systems encompass various types designed to enhance building aesthetics, insulation, and weather resistance. Ventilated façades feature an air gap that promotes airflow, reducing heat buildup and moisture accumulation. Curtain walls are non-load-bearing systems that provide visual continuity and weather protection, integrating glass or other materials. Non-ventilated façades lack an airflow mechanism, offering simpler installation but potentially reduced thermal efficiency. Each type serves distinct architectural needs while contributing to energy efficiency and building durability.</p></p>
