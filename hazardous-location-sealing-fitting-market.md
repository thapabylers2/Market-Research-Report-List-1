<p><p>The hazardous location sealing fitting market is divided into two main types: horizontal and vertical sealing fittings. Horizontal sealing fittings are designed to provide effective ingress protection and prevent the passage of hazardous materials in environments where components are installed parallel to the ground. In contrast, vertical sealing fittings are tailored for applications where components are positioned upright, ensuring that any fluids or debris cannot travel along the fitting. Both types are crucial for maintaining safety and compliance in hazardous environments.</p></p>
