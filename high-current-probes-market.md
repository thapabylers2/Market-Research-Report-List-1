<p><ul><li>Push-in</li><li>Screw-in</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high current probes market can be categorized into two primary types: push-in and screw-in probes. Push-in probes utilize a spring-loaded mechanism for easy connection, enabling quick and secure measurement of high current levels. They are favored for applications requiring rapid setup and removal. In contrast, screw-in probes provide a more robust and stable connection, ideal for environments with vibrations or where long-term measurements are needed. Both types serve critical roles in various electrical testing applications, ensuring accurate current readings.</p></p>
