<p><p>The hip and knee implants market is segmented into three main types: Primary Implants, Partial or Unicompartmental Implants, and Revision Implants. Primary implants are designed for initial joint replacement surgeries, providing full support. Partial or unicompartmental implants are used to replace only damaged parts of the joint, preserving healthy tissue. Revision implants are intended for patients who require replacement of previously implanted devices, typically due to wear or complications, ensuring continued joint functionality and pain relief.</p></p>
