<p><strong>The HVAC Chillers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Screw Chillers</li><li>Scroll Chillers</li><li>Centrifugal Chillers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC chillers market consists of three main types: screw chillers, scroll chillers, and centrifugal chillers. Screw chillers use rotary screws for efficient cooling, suited for large commercial applications. Scroll chillers employ spiral designs for compactness and quieter operation, ideal for medium to small systems. Centrifugal chillers leverage high-speed rotating impellers, delivering significant cooling capacity, often used in large buildings or industrial settings. Each type offers unique efficiency, capacity, and application advantages based on cooling needs.</p></p>
