<p><p>The HVAC Energy Recovery Ventilator market consists mainly of two types: Independent and Integrated models. Independent type systems function standalone, effectively exchanging indoor and outdoor air while recovering energy to maintain efficiency. They are suitable for retrofitting in existing buildings. Integrated type systems, on the other hand, are built into the HVAC system itself, offering seamless operation and better efficiency through direct integration with heating and cooling components. Both types enhance indoor air quality while reducing energy consumption.</p></p>
