<p><p>The HVAC VRV (Variable Refrigerant Volume) market is primarily categorized into two systems: Heat Recovery and Heat Pump. </p><p>Heat Recovery Systems allow simultaneous heating and cooling in different zones, optimizing energy efficiency and usage, particularly in commercial settings. </p><p>Heat Pump Systems, on the other hand, provide either heating or cooling, typically used in residential applications. They transfer heat from outside to inside during winter and vice versa in summer, ensuring reliable climate control while maintaining energy efficiency.</p></p>
