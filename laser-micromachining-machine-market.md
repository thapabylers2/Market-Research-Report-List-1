<p><p>The laser micromachining market comprises various types of lasers, including UV and green lasers. UV lasers operate at shorter wavelengths, providing high precision and minimal thermal impact, making them ideal for delicate materials like plastics and thin films. Green lasers, with a longer wavelength than UV, offer versatility in processing metals and organic materials, producing finer features and enhancing efficiency. Both types cater to industries such as electronics, medical devices, and automotive, driving innovations in micro-manufacturing techniques.</p></p>
