<p><ul><li>Flat Face</li><li>Conical Face</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The low profile solenoids market can be categorized into two main types: flat face and conical face solenoids. Flat face solenoids feature a minimalist design, providing a compact and efficient solution for applications with constrained space. They are often used in automotive and industrial sectors. In contrast, conical face solenoids have a tapered structure that allows for enhanced force generation and more effective actuation. This design is suitable for applications requiring higher responsiveness and power while maintaining a low profile.</p></p>
