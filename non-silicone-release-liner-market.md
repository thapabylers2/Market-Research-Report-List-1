<p><p>The non-silicone release liner market includes two primary types: release liner paper and release liner film. Release liner paper is typically made from pulp and coated with specialized chemicals to provide a smooth, release surface, making it ideal for labels and adhesive applications. In contrast, release liner film is made from plastic materials like polyethylene or polypropylene, offering enhanced durability and moisture resistance. Both types serve to protect adhesive surfaces during storage and application, ensuring optimal performance in various industries.</p></p>
