<p><ul><li>Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS)</li><li>Viscosupplements</li><li>Bone Graft Substitute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Orthobiologics encompasses various treatments aimed at enhancing healing processes in musculoskeletal conditions. Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS) utilize electrical or ultrasonic signals to promote bone regeneration and repair fractures. Viscosupplements are hyaluronic acid-based injections that alleviate joint pain by improving synovial fluid viscosity, thus enhancing joint mobility in osteoarthritis patients. Bone Graft Substitutes serve as alternatives to traditional bone grafting, aiding in bone regeneration and healing by providing scaffolding and vital growth factors, addressing various orthopedic needs.</p></p>
