<p><p>The perfume market comprises various types, each defined by fragrance concentration and longevity. Parfum has the highest concentration (20-30%) and lasts longer, while Eau de Parfum contains 15-20% fragrance and offers a balanced scent. Eau de Toilette is lighter (5-15%) and ideal for daily wear. Eau de Cologne features a lower concentration (2-5%) with a refreshing quality, often used in aftershaves. Eau Fraiche is the lightest, with around 1-3% fragrance, providing a subtle and fleeting scent experience.</p></p>
