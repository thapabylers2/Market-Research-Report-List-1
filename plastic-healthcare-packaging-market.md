Glass offers excellent protection and is often used for pharmaceuticals due to its inert nature. Paperboard, while less common for liquid containment, is utilized for primary and secondary packaging, such as cartons and blister packs, providing an eco-friendly option. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring product safety and integrity.</p></p>
