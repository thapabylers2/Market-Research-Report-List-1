<p><strong>The Pneumatic Brakes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Shaft Pneumatic Brakes</li><li>Disc Pneumatic Brakes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pneumatic brakes market is categorized primarily into two types: shaft pneumatic brakes and disc pneumatic brakes. Shaft pneumatic brakes utilize air pressure to engage braking mechanisms directly attached to a rotating shaft, providing efficient stopping power in machinery. Disc pneumatic brakes, on the other hand, operate by applying pressure to a disc, creating friction to slow or halt movement. Both types are widely used in various applications, including industrial machinery, vehicles, and railway systems, due to their reliability and effectiveness.</p></p>
