<p><p>The Portable Oil in Water Analyzer market features various technologies, primarily Infrared Spectrophotometry and UV Fluorescence Detection. Infrared Spectrophotometry measures the absorption of infrared light by oil droplets in water, providing quantitative analysis of oil concentrations. In contrast, UV Fluorescence Detection identifies oil through its fluorescent properties when exposed to ultraviolet light, offering sensitivity for detecting low oil levels. Both methods enable real-time monitoring and are essential for environmental compliance and safety in industries such as oil and gas, wastewater management, and environmental protection.</p></p>
