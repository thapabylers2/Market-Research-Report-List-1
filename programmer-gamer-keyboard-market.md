<p><ul><li>Mechanical Keyboards</li><li>Membrane Keyboards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmer and gamer keyboard market primarily consists of mechanical and membrane keyboards. Mechanical keyboards use individual switches under each key, offering tactile feedback, durability, and faster response times, making them popular for both programming and gaming. In contrast, membrane keyboards rely on a pressure pad system, which is typically quieter and more affordable but may lack the tactile feel and longevity of mechanical options. Gamers often prefer mechanical for performance, while programmers might choose based on comfort and typing experience.</p></p>
