<p><p>Restoration anchor markets comprise three primary types: Mechanical Restraining Anchors, Mechanical Repair Anchors, and Helical Tie Anchors. Mechanical Restraining Anchors are used to stabilize structures by providing lateral and vertical support. Mechanical Repair Anchors assist in repairing and reinforcing damaged structures, enhancing their load-bearing capacity. Helical Tie Anchors are installed by drilling into the ground, providing high-tensile support for stabilizing walls and foundations. Together, these anchors play a crucial role in strengthening and restoring compromised structures.</p></p>
