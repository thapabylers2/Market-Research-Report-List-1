<p><p>The riveted gratings market is divided into two primary types: smooth surface and blade surface. Smooth surface gratings feature a flat design ideal for pedestrian areas, offering a sleek appearance and ease of cleaning. In contrast, blade surface gratings have raised, serrated patterns that enhance slip resistance, making them suitable for industrial applications where safety is paramount. Both types are used in various sectors, including construction, transportation, and manufacturing, catering to specific functional and aesthetic requirements.</p></p>
