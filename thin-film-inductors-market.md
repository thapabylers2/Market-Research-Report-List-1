<p><p>Thin film inductors are categorized into two primary market types: SMD (Surface Mount Device) and Plug-in types. SMD inductors are designed for compact applications, allowing easy integration onto circuit boards with minimal space and high efficiency. They are widely used in modern electronics, benefiting from automated assembly processes. Plug-in inductors, on the other hand, are typically used in larger applications and offer straightforward installation. Both types serve unique roles in various industries, including telecommunications, automotive, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
