<p><ul><li>Macroscopic Urinalysis</li><li>Biochemical Urinalysis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urinalysis test market is categorized into two primary types: macroscopic urinalysis and biochemical urinalysis. Macroscopic urinalysis involves a visual examination of urine's physical characteristics, such as color, clarity, and odor, providing initial insights into a patient's health. In contrast, biochemical urinalysis employs chemical tests to analyze specific substances in urine, like proteins, glucose, and ketones, aiding in the diagnosis of various medical conditions. Together, these methods offer a comprehensive approach to assessing urinary health.</p></p>
