<p><p>Vertical baggers are automated machines used for packaging products in a vertical format, and they primarily fall into two market types: Continuous Motion and Intermittent Motion. </p><p>Continuous Motion vertical baggers operate seamlessly, producing bags without pauses, making them ideal for high-speed applications and ensuring minimal product loss. In contrast, Intermittent Motion vertical baggers pause between operations, allowing for greater flexibility in packaging various products and sizes, though typically at a slower pace. Both types cater to different production needs in industries like food, pharmaceuticals, and consumer goods.</p></p>
