<p><ul><li>Thermal Type</li><li>Athermal Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Waveguide Grating Routers (WGRs) are optical devices used for routing signals in communication systems. The market is primarily divided into two types: Thermal Type and Athermal Type. Thermal Type WGRs utilize temperature variations to achieve wavelength tuning, making them temperature-dependent. In contrast, Athermal Type WGRs are designed to maintain performance stability across temperature fluctuations, eliminating reliance on thermal changes. Both types serve critical roles in telecommunications, particularly for wavelength division multiplexing applications, each catering to specific operational requirements and environmental conditions.</p></p>
