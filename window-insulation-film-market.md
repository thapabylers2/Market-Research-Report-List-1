<p><p>The window insulation film market is segmented into crosslinked and non-crosslinked types. Crosslinked type films offer enhanced durability and thermal performance due to their chemically bonded structure, making them suitable for high-demand applications. In contrast, non-crosslinked type films are typically more flexible and easier to install, appealing to residential users seeking cost-effective solutions. Both types serve the purpose of improving energy efficiency in homes and buildings by reducing heat loss and enhancing indoor comfort, catering to diverse consumer preferences.</p></p>
