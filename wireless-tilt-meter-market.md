<p><ul><li>Buried Type</li><li>Installed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless tilt meter market comprises two main types: buried and installed. Buried tilt meters are embedded underground to monitor soil movement and structural stability, making them ideal for applications in civil engineering and geotechnical environments. Installed tilt meters, on the other hand, are mounted on external surfaces or structures, providing real-time monitoring of tilt changes in buildings and bridges. Both types employ wireless technology for data transmission, enhancing ease of use and reducing the need for extensive wiring.</p></p>
