<p><p>The wool wax market consists of two primary types: deoiled wool wax and conventional wool wax. Conventional wool wax, containing natural lanolin, retains its greasy texture and is often used in industrial applications, leather conditioning, and moisturizing products. Both types cater to distinct market needs based on their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
